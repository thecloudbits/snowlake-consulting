Data Center Design Consultancy

From greenfield builds to existing facility upgrades — we deliver end-to-end data center consultancy covering physical layout, power, cooling, networking, and security.

Our Data Center Design Expertise

A well-designed data center is the foundation of your entire IT operation. Poor design leads to cooling failures, power inefficiencies, and difficult-to-manage cabling that costs you for years. Snowlake Consulting brings the expertise to get it right the first time — whether you're building a new facility, expanding an existing one, or moving to a co-location provider.

As an authorized Dell Technologies partner, we combine vendor-agnostic consultancy with access to enterprise hardware at competitive pricing, ensuring your data center is both cost-effective and best-in-class.

Design Disciplines

  • Physical Layout & Space Planning — Rack placement, hot/cold aisle containment, and cable management design.
  • Power Design — UPS sizing, PDU layout, dual-feed distribution, and generator integration.
  • Cooling Design — CRAC/CRAH sizing, airflow modelling, and in-row cooling solutions.
  • Network Architecture — Spine-leaf topology, cabling standards, and structured cabling design.
  • Physical Security — Access control, CCTV, and biometric entry design recommendations.
  • DCIM Integration — Data center infrastructure management systems for real-time visibility.

Project Phases

  • Phase 1 — Requirements & Assessment — Current state review, capacity requirements, growth projections, and budget framing.
  • Phase 2 — Conceptual Design — High-level design options with cost-benefit analysis for each approach.
  • Phase 3 — Detailed Design — Full technical design documentation, bill of materials, and vendor specifications.
  • Phase 4 — Implementation Support — On-site oversight during build, rack & stack, and commissioning.
  • Phase 5 — Handover & Documentation — As-built documentation, operational runbooks, and staff training.

Hardware Partners

As an authorized Dell Technologies partner, we specify and procure Dell PowerEdge servers, PowerStore storage, PowerSwitch networking, and APC power infrastructure — with full support entitlements included.
