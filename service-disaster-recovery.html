Disaster Recovery & Backup Services

When disaster strikes, your ability to recover quickly determines whether your business survives. We build, test, and maintain DR solutions that bring you back online — fast.

Why Disaster Recovery Can't Be an Afterthought

Hardware fails. Humans make mistakes. Natural disasters happen. Ransomware strikes. Every organization faces the possibility of a critical IT failure — the question is whether you've prepared to recover. Snowlake Consulting helps you build a disaster recovery capability that matches your business's actual risk tolerance and recovery requirements.

We don't just set up backups — we design, document, and test complete DR solutions so that when the moment comes, your team knows exactly what to do and can execute under pressure.

Our DR Services

  • DR Strategy & Planning — Business impact analysis, RTO/RPO definition, and DR architecture design.
  • Backup Architecture Design — On-premises, cloud, and hybrid backup solutions tailored to your data volumes and retention requirements.
  • Replication Solutions — Continuous or scheduled replication of VMs, databases, and files to a secondary site or cloud.
  • DR Runbooks — Step-by-step documented recovery procedures for each critical system and application.
  • DR Testing — Scheduled failover tests in isolated environments to validate recovery procedures without impacting production.
  • Annual DR Exercises — Facilitated full DR simulations with documented results and improvement plans.
  • Cloud DR (DRaaS) — AWS-based disaster recovery with automated failover using CloudEndure or VMware SRM.

RTO & RPO — Understanding Your Targets

RTO (Recovery Time Objective) — How quickly must you be back online after a failure?

RPO (Recovery Point Objective) — How much data can you afford to lose (measured in time)?

We start every DR engagement by helping you define realistic, business-aligned RTO and RPO targets, then design a solution that achieves them within your budget.
