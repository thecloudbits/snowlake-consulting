Managed IT Services

Focus on your business while we handle your technology. 24/7 monitoring, proactive maintenance, and expert support for your entire IT environment.

What Are Managed IT Services?

Managed IT Services means handing the day-to-day responsibility of your IT infrastructure to a trusted partner. Snowlake monitors, manages, and maintains your environment around the clock — so issues are resolved before they become problems, and your team can focus on what they do best.

From servers and networks to cloud workloads and endpoints, we cover your entire stack under one flexible service agreement. No hidden costs, no surprise bills — just reliable IT that works.

What's Included

  • 24/7 Infrastructure Monitoring — Real-time alerts and automated responses to keep your systems online.
  • Proactive Patching & Maintenance — Scheduled updates applied during agreed maintenance windows, never during business hours.
  • Help Desk Support — Tier 1–3 support for your end users via phone, email, and remote access.
  • Incident Response — Rapid response SLAs with defined escalation paths for critical outages.
  • Capacity Planning — Monthly reviews to anticipate growth and prevent performance bottlenecks.
  • Monthly Reporting — Transparent reports on uptime, incidents, changes, and performance trends.
  • Security Management — Patch compliance, access reviews, and threat monitoring included.
  • Vendor Management — We liaise with your technology vendors on your behalf.

Environments We Manage

  • On-premises servers and network infrastructure
  • VMware vSphere and Nutanix environments
  • AWS cloud workloads and services
  • Red Hat Linux and OpenShift platforms
  • Backup and disaster recovery systems
  • Firewalls, switches, and network devices

Service Level Options

We offer tiered managed service packages to fit organizations of all sizes — from a 50-person business to an enterprise with multiple sites. All packages include a dedicated account manager and monthly review calls.
