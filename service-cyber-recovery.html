Cyber Recovery Services

Your last line of defence against ransomware and cyber attacks. Air-gapped vaults, immutable backups, and tested cyber recovery capabilities — so you can recover even when everything else fails.

Why Standard Backup Is Not Enough

Traditional backup solutions were designed to recover from hardware failures and human error — not sophisticated cyber attacks. Modern ransomware actively seeks out and encrypts or destroys backup repositories before attacking production systems. If your backups are connected to your network, they are vulnerable.

Cyber recovery goes further: we build isolated, air-gapped environments where a clean copy of your critical data is stored completely out of reach of attackers — and we validate that you can actually recover from it when it matters most.

Our Cyber Recovery Services

  • Cyber Vault Design & Deployment — Isolated recovery environments using Dell EMC PowerProtect Cyber Recovery or Commvault — air-gapped and hardened.
  • Immutable Backup Policies — Write-once, read-many (WORM) backup storage that cannot be modified or deleted — even by admins.
  • Air-Gap Configuration — Network isolation with automated vault lock/unlock schedules to limit the attack window.
  • Data Integrity Scanning — Automated malware scanning of backup copies inside the vault before they are trusted for recovery.
  • Ransomware Recovery Planning — Documented recovery playbooks for ransomware scenarios, including clean-room recovery procedures.
  • Recovery Testing — Regular recovery exercises to validate that your cyber vault data can actually be restored to a clean environment.
  • FileCloud Integration — Secure file storage and sharing with versioning and ransomware protection as an authorized FileCloud partner.

The 3-2-1-1-0 Rule

Modern cyber resilience follows the 3-2-1-1-0 backup rule — and we help you achieve it:

  • 3 copies of your data
  • 2 different storage media types
  • 1 copy offsite
  • 1 copy air-gapped / offline / immutable
  • 0 errors on recovery verification

Technologies We Deploy

  • Dell EMC PowerProtect Cyber Recovery
  • Dell PowerProtect Data Manager
  • Commvault Complete Backup & Recovery
  • Veeam with immutable repositories (S3 Object Lock)
  • FileCloud with ransomware protection (authorized partner)
  • AWS S3 Object Lock for cloud-based immutable backup
