Private Cloud Consulting

Design, deploy, and operate secure private cloud infrastructure that gives you the flexibility of the cloud with the control of on-premises — tailored to your workloads and budget.

What Is Private Cloud Consulting?

A private cloud gives your organization dedicated infrastructure — compute, storage, and networking — managed solely for your benefit. Unlike public cloud, you control your data, security policies, and performance. Our consultants help you design and deploy the right private cloud architecture, whether on-premises, in a co-location facility, or as a hybrid environment.

Snowlake Consulting brings deep expertise in VMware vSphere, Nutanix, and Red Hat OpenStack to deliver private cloud solutions that are scalable, secure, and cost-effective. We work with you from initial assessment through deployment and ongoing management.

Our Approach

We follow a structured methodology to ensure your private cloud meets current needs and scales with your business:

  • Discovery & Assessment — Workload analysis, capacity planning, and business requirements gathering.
  • Architecture Design — Custom cloud blueprint covering compute, storage, networking, and security.
  • Deployment & Integration — Hands-on implementation with full testing and validation.
  • Knowledge Transfer — Training your team to operate and manage the environment confidently.
  • Ongoing Support — Managed services and monitoring options post-deployment.

Key Deliverables

  • Private cloud architecture design document
  • VMware vSphere or Nutanix cluster deployment
  • Software-defined networking (NSX or OVN)
  • Shared or dedicated storage (vSAN, NFS, iSCSI)
  • Identity & access management integration
  • Security hardening and compliance baseline
  • Monitoring & alerting setup
  • Full operational runbooks

Technologies We Work With

  • VMware vSphere, vCenter, vSAN, NSX
  • Nutanix AHV & Prism
  • Red Hat OpenStack Platform
  • Dell PowerEdge & PowerStore (authorized partner)
  • Hybrid cloud connectivity (AWS, Azure)
